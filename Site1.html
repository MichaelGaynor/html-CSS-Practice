GOJIRA

Gojira is a French heavy metal band formed in 1996 (Though they went by "Godzilla" until 2001). Famous for driving drums and environmentally conscious lyrics the band has exploded from relative obscurity to somewhat-less-relative renown. Check out their website for information, or watch them perform!

BRUJERIA

Brujeria is a Mexican-American extreme metal band from Los Angeles. Formed in 1989, they portray themselves as wanted Drug Lords and are usually depicted covering their faces to avoid recognition by the FBI. Watch them here!

PANTERA

Pantera needs very little in the way of introduction. Famous for their heavy sound they are one of the undisputed kings of heavy metal and have been since near their beginnings in 1981. Visit their website or just headbang to this sick noise!
